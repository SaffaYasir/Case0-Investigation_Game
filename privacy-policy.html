1.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register with the application, including:

  • Email address (for account creation and authentication)
  • Display name (detective alias for in-game use)
  • Authentication tokens (for login services)

1.2 Game Data

We collect information about your gameplay to provide and improve our services:

  • Progress and save data ENCRYPTED
  • Case completion status and investigation progress
  • Game statistics and performance scores
  • Achievements and detective ranks earned
  • In-app purchase records and transaction history
  • Game settings and preferences

1.3 Technical Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use our application:

  • Device type, model, and manufacturer
  • Operating system version and platform
  • Application version and build number
  • Crash reports and error logs for debugging
  • Performance metrics and usage statistics
  • Network information (connection type, IP address)

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

Purpose Information Used

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Local Storage Encryption: All locally stored game data is encrypted
  • Secure Server Connections: HTTPS/SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular Security Audits: Periodic review and improvement of security measures
  • Access Controls: Strict authentication and authorization mechanisms
  • Data Anonymization: Where possible, data is anonymized for analysis
  • Firewall Protection: Network-level security for our servers
Note: While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for children under 13 years of age:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information
  • Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided us with personal information should contact us immediately

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary:

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained while your account is active

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

7.1 Access and Correction

  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate information

7.2 Deletion and Restriction

  • Right to Restrict: Request restriction of processing

7.3 Portability and Objection
